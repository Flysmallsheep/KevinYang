What's on

The SupSkier Club is Australia's oldest and largest snowsports club, with over 300,000 members from across the country, and is the respected and independent voice of the Australia ski industry. More than 2.5 million people in Australia go for a snowsports holiday each winter season. Whether it's the planning, booking or enjoying of your holiday, the SupSkier Club offers high-quality advice as well as informative content and assistance to ensure you get the most out of the mountains.

Our 300,000+ SupSkier Club members are loyal, highly interactive and extremely invested in the snowsports industry. Over 80% of our members have skied more than 20 weeks in total, always travelling to the mountains more than once in a winter season. SupSkier Club members' are with us because they are passionate about getting the most out of the mountains.
